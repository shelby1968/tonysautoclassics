About Us 

Tony Azcue founded Tony's Auto Classics in 2001. His love for the classic cars and his desire to preserve them is his motivation. Tony's mission is to provide the highest quality service and workmanship to all classic owners, whether it is a car, a truck or a motorcycle.

 

Tony is a University of Texas graduate in Electrical Engineering. He has been a classic Mustang owner since 1967. He has parallel careers in Avionics, Communications, Telecommunications and Manufacturing. 

 

He has been a member of the Mustang Club of America since 1988, the Shelby American Automobile Club since 2002 and the local clubs of Mustangs of East Texas & NTMC for several years. 

 

Tony's first Mustang in his collection is a 1966 Coupe that his brother Ray rescued from a Texas field and restored it. He had so much attention and admiration from driving this car, that he began to improve on the restoration and eventually brought it to the level of "Concours Trailered". His collection consist of a 1965 Black Fastback, a 1966 Signal Flare Red Coupe, a 1966 White Fastback (Shelby replica) and a 1968 Candy Apple Red Shelby GT-350.

 

The 1965 Black 2+2 has competed at MCA, IACA, NTMC, MET and other local shows. It has won numerous 1st place awards in Texas and other states. Participating in all of these shows throughout the years has allowed me to meet many wonderful people of which some of them have become good friends.
