Plastic Chrome Plating

Plastic rechroming is done with a vacuum metalized process. Most plastic pieces are normally repairable at an extra nominal cost. Plastic parts and panels are first stripped and thoroughly cleaned. Broken tabs and other repairs are first done. Followed by removal of all scratches and other minor flaws. The next steps are various levels of sanding and polishing of plastic to a mirror look finish. Then finally the pieces are thoroughly cleaned prior to the re-chroming process.
